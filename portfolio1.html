Reddit Rapid Research

Usability Testing Program, Reddit

I was inspired to create this project with classmates Maxine Hood and Barakah Quader after one of my friends suspected that her drink was spiked on a night out, but we had no way of confirming or otherwise checking. Drink tampering is unfortunately a prevalent problem in society today, and especially affects college-age women and other vulnerable communities.

We iteratively designed, developed and implemented a low intrusion prototype called Lit Lids that consists of a reusable smart lid and paired bracelet. Our tangible interface alerts users through light and vibrotactile feedback in the bracelet, preventing and detecting drink tampering via Bluetooth tracking along with pressure and motion sensors in the lid.
