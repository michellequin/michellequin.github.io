Vet Tech Persona

June - August 2020, Chewy

As a UX Research intern at Chewy, I was embedded on the Pet Health team. For my primary project, I developed a veterinarian technician, or vet tech, persona to help the Product Design team better understand and design pet health services and tools. Historically, the focus was on pet owners as users and not on pet health providers, meaning that there was a knowledge gap as Chewy entered the pet health and pharmacy space. Vet techs play a crucial front-facing role in vet clinics and need to be considered when designing for the pet health experience. I created a comprehensive representation of the vet tech experience and pain points after doing extensive background research, shadowing vets, and conducting user interviews with vet techs across the United States.

In addition to the vet tech persona work, I completed various other projects that included both qualitative and quantitative methodologies: a navigation card sort study, page purchase flow usability testing, NPS exploration and whitepaper, QX benchmark scoring, and strategic work to determine the name and direction for an unreleased product.
