Columbia University Design System

Columbia University I.T. Department (CUIT)

As a UX Researcher (and UX Designer, UX Engineer, UX Strategist, Operations Manager, and basically UX jack-of-all-trades) for Columbia University's IT department, I ran user testing on Columbia University's internal admin system, analyzed the usability of and redid Columbia University's public websites, and updated the Columbia Design System to be more accessible, uniform, and streamlined.

As one of two UX team members, I took the initiative towards establishing and improving a UX presence at CUIT, developing and launching a CUIT UX website, starting a research and design repository, and developing research guidelines and recommendations.

I also taught myself to use Figma, reproducing the updated Columbia Design System as easy-to-use Figma components, creating over a hundred components as well as establishing typeface and colors for standardized, accessible usage across university mobile and desktop platforms. Components include responsive animations and/or allow for customization where appropriate.

Tools used

  • Figma
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • Drupal 8
