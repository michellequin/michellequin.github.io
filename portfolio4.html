Belonging & ModMood

Measuring Sense of Belonging and Moderator Satisfaction, Reddit

For the Belonging project:

Reddit’s Mission is to “Bring community and belonging to everyone in the world.” Belonging is referred to at Reddit as the value that users get, but hadn't been qualified or quantified prior to this project. This multi-staged research project uncovered definitions for "belonging" on Reddit and metrics to measure sense of belonging on Reddit. The term belonging has been studied and unpacked extensively by academics, so this project began with a literature review and collaboration with external research experts at academic institutes and labs. Scale items were compiled and narrowed down to develop a Reddit-specific questionnaire. Qualitative user interviews were conducted on users who belong to a variety of communities, and the themes discovered from these interviews were used to finalize a Belonging survey.

For the ModMood project:

The Community team at Reddit sought to construct a "ModMood" metric for Reddit to measure how moderators feel about moderating on Reddit. Moderators are instrumental in keeping the platform up and running. The goal was to keep a pulse on and quantify how moderators feel and their general involvement with Reddit. This multi-staged research project followed a similar process to the Belonging project, concluding with surveys launched. My involvement in both projects was up until survey launch, and included giving targeted team readouts as well as company-wide presentations on academic insights, initial findings, and definitions around user belonging and moderator satisfaction.
