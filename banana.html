Michelle's Banana Bread

Congratulations! You've found my secret recipe

(Developed, perfected and beloved in my time across the US, China, Germany and England)


Bake time: 45-55 minutes

(Makes one 8x4 or 9x5-inch loaf pan)

Ingredients:

  • 4 bananas
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup (66g) granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup (66g) brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) oil or butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) milk or yogurt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

    • Steps:

    1. Preheat oven to 350F (177C). Grease a loaf pan.
    2. In a large bowl, mash the bananas using a fork. Beat in eggs one at a time until incorporated.
    3. Beat in granulated sugar and brown sugar, then beat in oil and milk until well combined. Mix in vanilla extract.
    4. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. Slowly fold dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined (don't overmix!).
    5. Spread evenly into the loaf pan, then bake 45-55 minutes, or until the outside is golden and the inside is set (a toothpick should come out clean).
    6. Let cool, then enjoy This banana bread tastes even better the next day and keeps well on the counter for up to a week (if it lasts that long!).
